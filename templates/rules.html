The Rules

  • $10 buy in gets you a team of 5 houseguests.
  • No person in the pool may have the exact same 5 houseguests as another.
  • Houseguests combinations are picked on a first come, first serve basis.
  • Buy in must be paid in cash or email money transfer before the show ends.

Point System

Each Houseguest on your team earns one point for every:

  • Head of Household they win.
  • Power of Veto they win.
  • Eviction they survive.

The season winner gets 1 bonus point for winning at the end.

Philippe & his Brother

While only one of them is allowed to compete a week, Philippe gets a point if either of them wins. If at any point both are in a competition together you only get a point if phillip wins.
