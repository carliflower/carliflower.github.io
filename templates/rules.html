The Rules

  • $10 buy in gets you a team of {[{vm.DataService.teamSize}]} houseguests.
  • No person in the pool may have the exact same {[{vm.DataService.teamSize}]} houseguests as another.
  • Houseguests combinations are picked on a first come, first serve basis.
  • Since Houseguests are not announced before the show starts this year, houseguests will be assigned in descending alphabetical order by first name once the show starts. Houseguest 1 being the closest first name to A, Houseguest 16 being the closest to Z.
  • Buy in must be paid no later then a week after the show starts.
  • When there is a tie for a prize the value is split equally.

Point System

Each Houseguest on your team earns one point for every:

  • Head of Household they win.
  • Power of Veto they win.
  • Eviction they survive.

The season winner gets 1 bonus point for winning at the end.
